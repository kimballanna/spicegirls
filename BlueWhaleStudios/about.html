About Us

We are a full service Makeup and Special Makeup Effects facility located in Orlando, Florida. From exquisite beauty and fashion makeup, to the most elaborate and cutting edge special makeup effects, to foam puppet and mascot construction, we can handle a wide variety of projects!

Our philosophy is simple:
Create a harmonic relationship with each client, and help to bring whatever it is you imagine to life!

We realize that we are part of a larger team, on every single project, and our goal is to meet all of your requirements, staying on time and in budget, yet never sacrificing quality. We do what we do because it is our passion, and we instill this passion into each and every project we take on.

Whether your budget is large or small, or your time severely limited, we are always willing to discuss your project with you!

Thank you for your consideration, and we look forward to working with you!
