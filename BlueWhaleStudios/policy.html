Shipping policy and charges

  • Standard Delivery ($15.00): 4-8 business days
  • Expedited Devliery ($25.00): 3 business days
  • Express Delivery ($35.00): 2 business days

International Shipping

We currently only ship within the contiguous United States. If you have any questions, please contact us: contact@bluewhalestudios.com.


Business days include Monday-Friday, excluding holidays. You will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number when your order is shipped.


Return Policy

At Blue Whale Studios, we want you to be completely satisfied with your order. If you are unsatisfied with your purchase we will refund your money, no questions asked. Refunds must be requested within 30 days of the original date of purchase, at which point the refund will be issued to the original form of payment. Please note shipping fees are non-refundable.


Steps for returning by mail:

  1. Fill out return form included in your packing slip and indicate reason for return.
  2. Pack items carefully.
  3. Attach return label included with invoice and keep tracking number for your records.
  4. You will be refunded within 1-2 weeks.

Tax Policy

By law, Blue Whale Studios is required to collect tax in all states where we have a physical location. Sales tax will be charged according to the destination your order is shipped to. If sales tax is applicable in your location, you will be charged according to the current rate in that state.
