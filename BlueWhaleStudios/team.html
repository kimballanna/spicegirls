Meet The Team

Background image for team section goes here

Jonah Levy

Jonah Levy, owner of Blue Whale Studios Inc., has been passionate about makeup and special makeup fx for most of his life. He has been working as a professional in the Makeup and Special F/X industry for over 21 years and has been part of makeup teams on such films as Get On Up, The Secret Life of Walter Mitty, The Butler, OZ: The Great and Powerful, Transformers 3, Green Lantern, & Zombieland as well as hit television shows such as The Walking Dead, Sleepy Hollow, Homeland, and Banshee to name a few. His skills include everything from beauty makeup and body painting to advanced prosthetic and creature design. He works and teaches nationally and internationally, and loves to share his passion with both amateurs and veterans alike. Jonah and his team at Blue Whale Studios inc put their all into everything they do and strive to deliver the absolute best quality for each and every project they take on!

Crystal Levy

Crystal Levy has been passionate about makeup for as long as she can remember. She is an established Makeup Artist that has worked on a variety of film and television projects, as well as commercials, photography shoots and weddings. She is co-owner and Lead Beauty Makeup Artist for Blue Whale Studios inc where she also assists her husband, Jonah Levy, on film and television sets, as well as live, corporate and theatrical events. Crystal loves to share her passion with others and has taught seminars across the country to inspire others who share a love for makeup.

Matt Sprunger

Professional makeup and special effects artist based out of Orlando, FL. Shop Supervisor at Blue Whale Studios, and member of the Local 798 Makeup Artist Union where he has worked on such films as "The Hunger Games," and "Windwalkers" and television shows such as "Sleepy Hollow", and has produced FX for "Banshee", "Helene Alone" and many more. After graduating in 2003 from the Tom Savini's Special Makeup Effects Program, Matt and his soon-to-be wife moved to Florida, where he began his career at Universal Studios Makeup department as well as M.A.C. Pro, and a variety of freelance projects. In 2011, Matt was hired on the Blue Whale Studios inc team full time and soon became Shop Supervisor where he has been instrumental in the creation of a wide variety of makeup fx, creature creations, prop fabrication, specialty costumes, and more.

Luandra Whitehurst

Luandra Whitehurst is a member of the Local 798 with over 10 years of experience working in Theme Parks, on Commercials, and in Film and Television. She has worked on films and shows such as "The Hunger Games: Mockingjay", "The Walking Dead", and "Miss Peregrine's Home for Peculiar Children". She is a multifaceted artist with a broad skill set including beauty, corrective, and makeup effects with a strong passion for sculpting and creating realistic and elaborate character makeups. She started her career by working on Independent films and in the Makeup Department at Universal Studios which is where she began working with Jonah Levy and Matt Sprunger. As projects at Blue Whale Studios inc continued to grow, her skills were utilized in the shop and on set application. In addition, LuAndra teaches at Cosmix, School of Make-up Artistry in South Florida and has written curriculum for their Special FX Program.
